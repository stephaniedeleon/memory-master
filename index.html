Memory Master

Welcome challenger! Do you think you got what it takes to become a Memory Master?

General Rules:

  • You will be given a pattern, and after each sequence, your task is to repeat it by clicking on the buttons.
  • For each turn you only have 10 seconds to repeat the pattern given. So act quickly!
  • You have 3 chances. If you make a mistake, pick up where you left off (do not start from the beginning).
  • Good luck and have fun!
